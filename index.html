Telangana is the newly formed states in India. It was carved out of Andhra Pradesh on June 2nd 2014. The newly formed state comprises of 112,077 square kilometers and constitutes of a population of 35,193,978 as per the latest census in 2011. By the size of its land mass, Telangana is the twelve largest states in the country. The well-known cities in Telangana are Hyderabad, Warangal, Secunderabad and Nizamabad. The state of Telangana has borders with Andhra Pradesh, Maharashtra, Karnataka and Chhattisgarh. Hindi, Urdu and telugu are the major languages in the state of Telangana.
